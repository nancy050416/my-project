02 / Craft

A measured process for objects that feel almost weightless.

01

Form

We tune wall thickness, rim angle, and base weight until a cup reads as quiet, precise, and balanced from every side.

02

Light

Each prototype is tested against white, daylight, and warm hospitality environments so the glass reflects the room instead of turning cloudy.

03

Edition

For brand launches and spatial programs, we build numbered runs with controlled geometry, engraving, and packaging systems.

04

Service

We advise on table setting, lighting, storage, and replacement logic so the object keeps its clarity in real use.
